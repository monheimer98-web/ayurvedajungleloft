Frequently asked questions

Detailed answers before you commit to your retreat.

Do you offer only fixed packages?

No. We offer 5/7/10-night structures and fully individualized formats from 3 nights onward.

Is 3 nights enough?

For a short reset, yes. For deeper therapeutic goals, 7+ nights are generally more effective.

Can couples have different treatment plans?

Yes. You can stay together while receiving separate protocols and meal adaptations.

Do you offer 14 or 21-night stays?

Yes, as optional extended formats for specific cases where deeper progression is useful.

What is included in the price?

Accommodation, consultations, daily therapies, yoga/breath sessions, therapeutic full-board and support planning.

Do you treat medical conditions?

We provide wellness-oriented Ayurvedic support. For serious medical conditions, external physician coordination may be required.

Do you provide airport transfers?

Yes. Private transfer from Colombo Airport can be arranged as part of your proposal.

Can I book just accommodation without Ayurveda?

Yes, subject to availability. You can also combine regular stay days and Ayurveda days.

Do you handle food allergies and intolerances?

Yes. Please share details in advance so the kitchen can tailor your menus safely.

Is there enough privacy on the property?

Yes. The Jungle Loft has only four units and intentionally low-density hospitality.

How fast do you reply to inquiries?

Usually within 24 hours with a personalized recommendation and pricing.

How do I secure my booking?

Either via our direct booking engine or through a custom payment link after consultation.
