The 4-stage care framework

How we turn Ayurveda principles into a precise guest journey.

Diagnose
Constitution assessment, symptom mapping, stress/sleep/digestion profile and treatment priorities.
Prescribe
Daily therapy sequence, meal plan and movement protocol matched to your baseline.
Adapt
Progress checks and protocol updates based on your day-by-day response.
Integrate
Realistic continuation plan for home, including routine and nutrition guidance.

Treatment categories we combine

  • Oil-based restorative therapies for nervous-system downregulation
  • Localized protocols for neck, back, joints and muscle tension patterns
  • Heat/steam and circulation-supporting techniques where indicated
  • Breath and movement modules to stabilize long-term recovery

Exact modalities and frequency are always physician-led and adjusted to your response.
