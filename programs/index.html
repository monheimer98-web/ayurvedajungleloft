Programs FAQ

Quick guidance for choosing duration and format.

Should I choose 5, 7 or 10 nights?

5 nights suit short strategic recovery, 7 nights are the best-balanced core option, and 10 nights provide deeper restoration and integration depth.

Are prices final?

Base rates are listed as orientation. Final pricing depends on room category, season and treatment intensity after intake.

Can I start with 5 nights and extend on site?

Usually yes, subject to room availability and medical recommendation after your first treatment phase.

Is 21 nights mandatory for serious results?

No. Many guests achieve strong outcomes within 7-10 nights. 14/21-night stays are optional for complex long-standing patterns.
