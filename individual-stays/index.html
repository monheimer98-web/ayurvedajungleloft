When does a custom stay make sense?

Especially useful when you have a very specific objective or limited travel time.

  • You can stay only 3-4 nights and want a focused medical reset
  • You travel as a couple with different therapeutic priorities
  • You want to combine retreat days with selected excursions
  • You need a pre- or post-program around an existing 7/10-night stay
Minimum Duration

3 Nights

Three nights work as a concentrated Discovery format. For deeper therapeutic outcomes, we typically recommend 7 nights and above.

How personal planning works

We keep the process simple but clinically structured.

Step 1
Pre-arrival health call with our retreat team to map goals and limitations.
Step 2
Initial physician diagnosis on arrival and final program architecture.
Step 3
Daily treatment and nutrition adjustments based on your response.
Step 4
Departure plan with realistic home integration and support options.

Examples of custom formats

3-night Recovery Booster, 4-night Sleep Regulation Focus, 6-night Couple Program with distinct treatment tracks, or a 12-night phased protocol with mid-stay adaptation.
